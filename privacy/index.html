Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Blumgi (“Service Provider”, “we” or “us”) collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users (“You”) of our mobile games (each, an “Application”).

1. Data Collection & Use

Blumgi does not collect or store any personal or device data. We rely solely on the following third-party services for any analytics or advertising data:

  • Apple App Store to distribute our iOS and macOS Applications. See: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Play Store to distribute our Android Applications. See: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob to serve ads. See : policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

2. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not share any data ourselves. Any information collected by AdMob, Play Services or the App Store is subject to those providers’ own policies and practices. They do not share it with us in personally identifiable form.

3. Opt-Out & Data Controls

On Android, you can reset or limit your Advertising ID by going to Settings > Google > Ads and choosing “Reset advertising ID” or “Opt out of Ads Personalization.”

On iOS you can limit ad tracking via your device’s Settings > Privacy > Tracking controls.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Applications are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from anyone under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we might have any data from children under 13, please email us at contact@blumgi.com.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Effective date” below. Continued use of the Application after changes means you accept the new policy.
